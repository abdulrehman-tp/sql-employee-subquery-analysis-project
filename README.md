# SQL Employee Subquery Analysis Project

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee salary data using SQL subqueries.  
It covers basic, intermediate, and advanced query techniques used in real-world data analysis.

The goal of this project is to build strong understanding of subqueries, correlated subqueries, and advanced filtering logic.

---

## Dataset Description

Table: emp_salary

Columns:
- emp_id
- emp_name
- department
- salary


 ### Interview-Level Questions
1. Show employees earning more than IT department average  
2. Show employees earning less than HR department average  
3. Show employees with second highest salary  
4. Show employees earning more than overall average but less than maximum salary 
5. Show employees earning more than their department average  
6. Show employees earning less than their department average  
7. Show employees who are highest paid in their department  
8. Show employees who are lowest paid in their department
9.  Show department with highest average salary  
10. Show departments where average salary is greater than overall average  
11. Show employees whose salary is greater than all employees in HR department  
12. Show employees whose salary is less than any employee in IT department
